In depth Analysis of Network Data

In this project, concluding my four year study program at the TU Munich, I had a deep dive into the Measurement Lab NDT dataset, containing internet speed test data from across the world. I analyzed the dataquality and classified parts of the data that are suitable or not suitable for analysis, I analyzed the transport and application layer limitations of the connections and I outlined different approaches for Congestion Control analysis and classification based on this dataset.

An Essay about the japanese societal pressure on the youth

How does optimzation pressure effect the human? With this question in mind this essay, written in German, contains an in depth analysis of the japanese educational society and the pressure it's youths are facing. It tries to create references between common sociologial theories about youth, develpment and aggression and the things happening in japan to explore one example of how optimization pressure can effect us.

German Rent Analysis

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Project date: Januar 2024
  • Project URL: View here

A Data Analysis about the German Rents

In Germany, many people complain about rising living costs, especially rising rents. Living and studying in Munich, I really feel the pain of high rent and how it affects living standards. But the real estate market is very intransparent, and I wanted precise numbers. So, I tried to understand how high rents in Germany actually are, how they developed over time, and why people complain about high rent prices. For this, I got some data from the “Statistisches Bundesamt” (Engl. Federal Statistical Office ) to analyze and visualize the development of the German rent indices.
